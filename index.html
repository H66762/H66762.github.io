A Lighter than air solution

The first ESCARGO product will be a 1 tonne payload airship operating on Hydrogen-Electric propulsion technology, with VTOL and Solar capability, aiming at the range of 2500 km, fully loaded with Helium as the lifting gas.

What we offer

Our Airship solves a lot of problems faced by the cargo industry.

  • Lower costs for the customer (Operators and End users)
  • Reduction of delivery time by at least 40%-50% when compared to trucks
  • Significantly lower carbon emissions
  • Lower maintenance costs
  • Oversized cargo flexibility
  • Point to Point delivery
  • Ability to access remote areas
  • Fewer infrastructural needs
  • Operating capability at night
